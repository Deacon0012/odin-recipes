Chocolate Mousse

Ingredients

  • 200g dark chocolate
  • 3 eggs
  • 50g sugar
  • 300ml cream

Instructions

  • Step 1: Melt the chocolate.
  • Step 2: Whisk the eggs and sugar together.
  • Step 3: Fold in the melted chocolate.
  • Step 4: Whip the cream and fold it in.
  • Step 5: Chill for 2 hours before serving.
