Cheesecake

Rich and creamy cheesecake recipe.

Ingredients

  • 1 1/2 cups graham cracker crumbs
  • 1/4 cup sugar
  • 1/2 cup butter, melted
  • 4 (8 ounce) packages cream cheese
  • 1 cup sugar
  • 1 teaspoon vanilla extract
  • 4 large eggs
  • 1 cup sour cream

Instructions

  • Step 1: Preheat oven to 325°F (160°C).
  • Step 2: Mix graham cracker crumbs, 1/4 cup sugar, and melted butter in a bowl.
  • Step 3: Press mixture into the bottom of a 9-inch springform pan.
  • Step 4: Beat cream cheese, 1 cup sugar, and vanilla until smooth.
  • Step 5: Add eggs one at a time, mixing well after each addition.
  • Step 6: Stir in sour cream until well combined.
  • Step 7: Pour filling over crust and smooth the top.
  • Step 8: Bake for 55-60 minutes or until set.
  • Step 9: Let cool before refrigerating for at least 4 hours before serving.
